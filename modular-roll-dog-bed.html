Modular Roll Dog Bed

This modular dog bed is designed for comfort, flexibility, and playfulness. Each roll is made from memory foam, giving it a responsive feel that adapts to the dog's body and creates a supportive resting surface.
The two outer cushions are connected with hidden magnets, allowing them to shift, fold, or move inward to form a raised headrest. This adjustable structure lets you shape the bed to your dog’s needs, whether they prefer a flatter surface or gentle elevation for their neck and head. The design also works well on sofas or any flexible furniture, keeping its shape while remaining soft and adaptable. The covers are fully washable.
