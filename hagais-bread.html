Haggai & the Bread

I designed a full-length vertical illustration that reflects the young, hipster vibe of the neighborhood while staying true to the bakery’s warm, whimsical identity. The artwork was created as one continuous piece for the façade, but also designed so it could break apart into individual illustrations to be used on different occasions.A key challenge was composing a variety of characters within a tall, narrow format. I resolved this by connecting them through a rising cloud of illustrated aroma from the fresh bread, creating a playful visual thread that unifies the entire piece.
