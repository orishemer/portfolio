Homonovus

This work explores the fascinating concept of human-animal hybrids, capturing the intersection of human aesthetics and natural forms through digital techniques. By integrating human and animal characteristics, the artwork delves into the artificial interplay between people and nature, revealing the complexities of our relationship with the natural world through a digital lens.

process:
01
3D Scanning
The process begins with 3D scanning of human subjects.Polygon Reduction: The scanned models are initially simplified by reducing the polygon count, which streamlines the complexity while preserving core attributes.
02
Hybridization
The simplified models are then transformed to integrate animal features, creating hybrid forms that juxtapose human aesthetics with natural elements.Texture Mapping: Custom textures are applied to the models, enhancing their visual depth and integrating natural patterns that reflect the hybrid nature of the forms.
03
Puzzleworks
Puzzle-Like ConstructsOpen-source software Blender is utilized to further refine and manipulate the models. The program is employed to generate intricate, puzzle-like patterns that can be assembled into cohesive hybrid figures. The digital prints are reconstructed into complete hybrid characters. This modular approach underscores the artificial nature of the fusion and allows for interactive assembly.
